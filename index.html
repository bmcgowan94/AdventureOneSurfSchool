ABOUT US

Adventure One opened its doors in March 2004. Originally located in Letterkenny, we were North Donegal’s only surf shop! In May 2006 we opened the 1st ISA Approved Surf School on Donegal’s North coast and haven’t looked back since. Being ISA approved means our instructors are qualified ISA level 2 coaches as well as RLSS beach lifeguards, and we are fully insured. Click here to see where you can find us.

What do we provide?

We provide lessons and coaching for surfers of all abilities. From beginner to advanced, and from age 8 upwards. We offer group packages, individual lessons, kids surf camps, board rentals, and much more.

If you are already familiar with surfing, why not join us on our Improver/Intermediate package, or if you want to give the kids something to remember the summer by, send them on our Kids Surf Camps package. We now also offer a Stand-Up Paddle Boarding package. This is a somewhat new craze, which uses a surf-style board and a long paddle. In some way it is a cross between canoeing and surfing... and very fun! We also work closely with a number of accommodation providers, so for the best deals please check out our surfing holiday packages. See our Packages here.
